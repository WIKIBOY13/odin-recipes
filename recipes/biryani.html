Biryani

Description

Biryani is a common dish among South Asian countries especially Pakistan and India.It is basically chicken and rice cooked up in special Masalas.

Try it and it will be worth your while.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Sindhi Biryani Masala
